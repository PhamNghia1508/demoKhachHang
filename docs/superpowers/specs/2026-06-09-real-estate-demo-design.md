# Real Estate Rental Demo Design

## Muc tieu

Tao mot website demo thuyet phuc khach hang ve kha nang xay dung nen tang tim va dang tin bat dong san cho thue. Demo dung du lieu gia lap, khong sao chep noi dung hoac thong tin lien he that tu website tham khao.

## Pham vi demo

- Trang chu hien dai, responsive, nhan dien thuong hieu rieng.
- Tim kiem theo tu khoa, khu vuc, loai bat dong san va muc gia.
- Danh sach tin noi bat co the loc va sap xep truc tiep.
- Modal chi tiet tin gom thu vien anh, thong so, tien ich va lien he.
- Form dat lich xem nha co thong bao xac nhan gia lap.
- Luong dang tin ba buoc de khach hang hinh dung trai nghiem chu nha.
- Bang pham vi san pham chia theo Demo, MVP va Mo rong.

## Phong cach

- Mau xanh navy tao cam giac uy tin, mau cam dung cho hanh dong chinh.
- Hinh anh bat dong san lon, bo cuc thoang, chu de doc.
- Noi dung tieng Viet, muc gia va khu vuc gan voi thi truong TP.HCM.

## Kien truc

Demo la ung dung frontend tinh gom HTML, CSS va JavaScript ES modules. Du lieu tin dang nam trong mot module rieng; bo loc la cac ham thuan de co the kiem thu bang Node.js ma khong can thu vien ngoai.

## Tieu chi hoan thanh

- Chay bang mot static server thong thuong.
- Bo loc, sap xep, modal chi tiet, form lien he va luong dang tin hoat dong.
- Hien thi tot tren desktop va mobile.
- Co bang tinh nang de ho tro tu van va bao gia cho khach.
